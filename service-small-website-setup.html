Small Website Setup

A Small Website Setup gives your business a clean, fast, and personal online presence. It’s ideal for anyone who wants a simple, professional website that works well and doesn’t need constant maintenance.

Beyond being clean and fast, I set you up with a structure that’s easy to keep tidy. You’ll get a short walkthrough video and a mini‑guide showing where everything lives, so you can make quick edits with confidence—no ongoing retainers required.

Why it matters

A lightweight stack, sensible page structure, and clear documentation mean fewer moving parts, faster load times, and changes you can make yourself.

  • Fast, secure setup with minimal upkeep.
  • Straightforward design focused on clarity and usability.
  • Adaptable structure ready for future growth.
  • Personalised to reflect your brand and message.
