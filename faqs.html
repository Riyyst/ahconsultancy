Business consultancy

What does AH Consultancy do?

We help organisations improve how work gets done. From clearer processes and documentation through to practical operational changes that reduce friction and improve consistency.

What types of organisations do you work with?

We support established organisations, teams, small, medium or large businesses across the UK or individuals.

Can you help standardise processes and documentation?

Yes! We create clear procedures (SOPs), operating guides, templates, and process maps that make day‑to‑day delivery easier and more consistent.

IT consultancy

What does IT consultancy include?

Practical guidance and support around business systems, tooling, information governance, documentation, process alignment, and improvement planning. Where needed, we can help scope changes and translate requirements into actionable steps.

Do you provide IT policies and governance documentation?

Yes! We can produce IT policies (alongside other policies) such as acceptable use, access control, security, and operational guidance aligned to how your organisation works.

Policies & compliance

Can you write GDPR and privacy documentation?

Yes! We create and refine documents ranging from privacy policies to data protection‑aligned policies. We can also help with policy packs covering information security and operational controls.

Do you provide legally focused policy documentation?

We provide policy documentation that supports common legal and compliance needs (for example privacy, acceptable use, and information security). We can help prepare structured drafts and documentation for your review.

Automation

What do you mean by automation?

Automation means reducing repeat manual work, for example: reliable workflows, data handling, reporting outputs, and process steps that can be standardised or scripted.

Do you build custom tools or scripts?

Yes! We can deliver lightweight automation and templates that fit your workflow, including repeatable reporting, document generation, and process tooling.

Data analysis & reporting

What can data analysis help with?

Data analysis helps you understand performance, risks, trends and opportunities. We can build reporting that makes decision‑making faster and clearer.

Do you create dashboards and KPI reporting?

Yes! We can design dashboards and reporting templates (including Excel reporting) that track key metrics and present them clearly.

Websites

Do you build business websites?

Yes! We build professional websites for organisations that need a clean online presence, with a focus on clarity, usability and performance.

Can you improve an existing website?

Yes! We can optimise structure, content clarity, performance and search visibility, all while keeping your brand and layout consistent.

How we work

Is AH Consultancy UK‑wide?

Yes! We're based in Suffolk and work with organisations across the UK.

Do you work remotely?

Yes! All of our engagements are handled remotely. This can be from communications over emails, phone calls, or video calls such as 'Teams' or 'Zoom' meetings.

How do we get started?

The best way to begin the proccess is a short discussion either by email or phone call to confirm targets, scope, priorities, and timelines. We'll respond with next steps as soon as we can!
