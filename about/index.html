About me

I work across a wide range of IT roles — from technical support to system administration, data analysis, and digital content management. My day-to-day work keeps me hands-on with both people and technology, solving problems and improving systems to make things run more efficiently. I first started in business administration and gradually moved into IT, discovering how the two overlap in improving operations and workflows. During COVID, I began taking on small personal projects that strengthened my technical skills, and that experience has since grown into a key part of my professional life. I now work as an IT Officer within an NHS GP practice, managing and developing systems that support staff and patient care. Outside of that, I’m also studying law. This allows me to expand from IT but also offer practical business templates, policies, and contracts that strengthen the operational side of work. For me, IT is both a career and a creative outlet. I focus on functionality, reliability, and innovation, tailoring every project to the specific goals and needs of the people I work with.

For more information on my personal projects, portfolio, and more information about me, please visit www.adrianheadley.com.

About AH Consultancy

Established in 2025, AH Consultancy is a solo venture owned and managed entirely by me created to help businesses and professionals work smarter (not harder). I provide tailored support across website development, workflow improvement and automation, data management, digital strategy, and business policy templates and documents. What makes AH Consultancy different is its personal hands-on approach. There’s no team to navigate or layers of communication. Just direct, reliable support from someone who understands both the technical side and the business impact of every decision. Each project is managed with care, precision, and a focus on real-world results. Unlike larger firms that often rely on rigid systems, AH Consultancy is flexible by design. I adapt to the way you work, shaping practical solutions that fit naturally into your daily operations. Whether you’re looking for a simple website, smarter digital tools, or a more structured business setup, you’ll get clear advice and dependable outcomes without unnecessary complexity. My goal is to see AH Consultancy grow steadily over time — evolving as opportunities arise — but always staying true to its roots: a straightforward, one-to-one consultancy that helps people and organisations improve the way they work.
