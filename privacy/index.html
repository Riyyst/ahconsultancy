Privacy

This page explains how AH Consultancy handles information when you use our website or our WhatsApp chatbot. We aim to keep things simple: we use analytics for anonymous, aggregated statistics only, and we do not store personal data from the chatbot.

Website privacy notice

Cookies

We do not use cookies on this website for marketing, profiling, or storing personal information.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand overall demand and improve the site , for example, how many visits we get, which pages are viewed most, and how people move through the site. This is used for figures and statistics only and is not used to identify you.

URL parameters (campaign tracking)

We may use URL parameters (for example, tracking tags on links) to understand how activity starts on the site , such as whether someone arrived via an advert, Google search, or another source. This data is used to measure performance and improve what we offer.

Personal data

AH Consultancy does not collect, store, or use personal data through this website for analytics purposes. Any analytics data we review is aggregated and statistical.

WhatsApp Chatbot

What the chatbot is for

The WhatsApp chatbot is there to help signpost you to the right place , such as information on specific services, how to get in touch, or where to find the right page on our site.

No storage or logs

The chatbot does not keep conversation logs. We do not store names, phone numbers, message content, or any other personal information from the chatbot.

WhatsApp processing

WhatsApp (Meta) will process messages and metadata as part of providing the WhatsApp service. AH Consultancy does not download or retain this information from the chatbot.
